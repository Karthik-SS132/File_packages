Mservice.Net is a mobile app designed for small and medium-sized companies. It's meant to help their field service and sales teams when they're out working. The app allows these teams to manage their activities and keep track of their work in a straightforward and easy-to-use way.

The app will be used for capturing all the technical parameters and updating checklists of machines in the service report. The service report will be in digital form. Capturing various photographs and attachment of scanned documents can be done from the app. Maintenance history of every equipment and dashboards can be accessed. Sales Engineers use for new equipment sales activities with meeting report capturing enquiry details and status update.
