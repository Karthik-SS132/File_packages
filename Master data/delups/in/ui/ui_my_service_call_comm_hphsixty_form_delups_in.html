HPH Series UPS start-up Procedure:Make sure all the breakers of the UPS are in off position.

Connect Safety earth (ground), Rectifier Input, Bypass Input, Battery Input and UPS output to the System and check tightness of all the terminations.

Make sure input neutral is connected to UPS at right position and Neutral should be connected to UPS directly not through any breaker/Change over Switch.

(Note: As per the safety regulation it is recommended to connect safety earth to the equipment before applying any power to the system)
